List of sizing for "responsiveness" or "responsive web sites"

320px — 480px: Mobile devices

481px — 768px: iPads, Tablets

769px — 1024px: Small screens, laptops

1025px — 1200px: Desktops, large screens

1201px and more —  Extra large screens, TV



Keep in mind that we will only use Mobile and iPad sizing (watch video for more detail) or use numbers below:
